# Code of Conduct

Refinery uses a simple contributor standard: be direct, respectful, and focused on the work. Harassment, personal attacks, doxxing, abuse, and discriminatory behavior are not acceptable in project spaces.

Voice cloning can be sensitive. Do not use project issues, pull requests, or discussions to request or share non-consensual voice cloning workflows, private voice samples, leaked recordings, or impersonation targets.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate these rules. Serious or repeated violations may result in being blocked from the project.

For broader language, see the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
